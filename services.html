OUR SERVICES

Custom Electronic Hardware Design & Development

At Tri-angle Simulation Pvt. Ltd, we specialize in custom electronic hardware design and development tailored to the unique requirements of our clients. Our experienced engineering team collaborates closely with clients to understand their specific needs, ensuring that the designed hardware integrates seamlessly with our simulation systems. This bespoke approach guarantees enhanced performance, reliability, and adaptability, enabling our clients to achieve optimal results in their engineering and industrial applications.

Custom Process Research & Development

Our commitment to innovation is reflected in our custom process research and development services. We leverage advanced methodologies and technologies to explore and develop tailored solutions that meet the evolving needs of the engineering and chemical industries. Our dedicated team conducts thorough research to identify improvements and efficiencies, ensuring that our clients stay at the forefront of technological advancements.

Custom Process Simulation Analysis

We offer comprehensive custom process simulation analysis to help clients visualize and optimize their processes. Utilizing sophisticated simulation tools, we analyze various scenarios to identify potential bottlenecks and inefficiencies. This analytical approach enables clients to make informed decisions, ultimately enhancing their operational efficiency and productivity.

Computer Based Training CBT Development

Our expertise extends to the development of computer-based training (CBT) programs designed to enhance learning and skill acquisition for engineers and technicians. These interactive training modules utilize our simulation systems, allowing users to gain practical experience in a controlled environment. By integrating real-world scenarios, we ensure that participants can apply their knowledge effectively in actual operational settings.

Custom Automation Process Development

Tri-angle Simulation Pvt. Ltd excels in custom automation process development, providing solutions that streamline operations and enhance efficiency. Our team collaborates with clients to design automation processes that meet their specific requirements, integrating advanced control systems and software to optimize performance across various applications.

Automate Existing mannual System PC-Based SCADA

We specialize in automating existing manual systems using PC-based SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) solutions. By modernizing legacy systems, we enhance operational visibility and control, allowing clients to monitor processes in real-time and improve decision-making. Our SCADA systems are designed for scalability and flexibility, ensuring they adapt to future needs.

On Site Training Of Power Refineries For Engineering & ITA Students

Tri-angle Simulation Pvt. Ltd is committed to knowledge transfer and skill development.
